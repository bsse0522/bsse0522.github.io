Our Story
3 Years

History of Loence dates back to July, 2016 when a group of BSSE-5th batch students from IIT,DU, decided to develop a generalized management system as their final semester project. Attracted by the complexity and immensity of an ERP system and realizing the need of high-quality:low-cost ERP support to small and medium businesses, they started developing an ERP for Aman Plastic Industries, a dominant player in plastic toy industries. In the mean time the team welcomed talented graduates from different dept of DU and BUET making themselves competent to handle the unique technical and marketing challenges implementation of an ERP in Bangladesh poses. On September, 2018 Loence delivered their first two Cloud ERPs to Aman Plastic Industries followed by Zihan Plastic Industries. The team continues to grow, attracting ambitious and determined talents from versatile backgrounds.
