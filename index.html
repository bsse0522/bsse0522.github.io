Business Opportunities
Reduction in operational cost

In a typical business, managers spend lots of their working hours traversing piles of documents to find the appropriate data. Automating most of these tasks, an ERP system contributes in a dramatic reduction in operational cost

Improved efficiency

With less time spent to carry out repetitive daily activities, a properly implemented ERP frees up your team to focus on revenue-affecting tasks, improving efficiency of the team.

Total visibility and collaboration

ERP allows data from every department easily accessible to every layer of your management which provides more coherent workflows and allows inter-departmental processes to be easily tracked with maximum efficiency.

Improved forecasting and decision making

ERP gives you the ability to analyze and compare functions across departments. Since the information within ERP is as accurate as possible, businesses can make realistic estimates and more effective forecasts and decisions.

Improved supply chain performance

A streamlined supply chain becomes better and more responsive via improved demand forecasting, inventory management, procurement and facilitates manufacturing innovations, which can help you reduce costs and develop exciting new products that give your business the competitive edge it needs to stay ahead.

Reduction in inventory cost

Proper implementation of ERP results in- 15-25 percent reduction in funds invested in safety stock, 10-25 percent fewer stock outs, for improved availability and productivity, 10-25 percent drop in administrative costs for replenishing inventory, 33-66 percent less resource time spent managing inventory.

Improved customer satisfaction

Your sales team will be able to focus on building and maintaining customer relationships and providing better customer interactions from targeted marketing instead of maintaining spreadsheets and books.

Scalability

Whether you’re expanding your customer base; entering new markets; rolling out new processes, departments or products; or are otherwise growing your business, adding new functionality to the ERP platform is easy with us.

Storing appropriate quantity of data

Loence ERP stores data efficiently so you can easily access and analyze your data to further optimize your business or to adopt new competitive strategies.

Data security

Prepared for any unwanted occurrences, we back up your data on regular basis. Our impenetrable security ensures your data is never accessed unauthorized whereas traditionally stored data may get damaged or lost and is vulnerable to accident and unauthorized access.
